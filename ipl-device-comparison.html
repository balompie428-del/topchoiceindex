How to Choose the Right IPL Device

  • Intensity levels: Adjustable settings help match comfort and skin sensitivity.
  • Skin compatibility: Ensures the device works safely for your skin type.
  • Treatment speed: Faster flash rates reduce time per session.
  • Ease of use: Simple controls and clear displays support consistent use.
  • Overall value: Balance of performance, features, and price.

What to Avoid

  • Limited intensity levels: Restricts comfort and flexibility during use.
  • Slow treatment time: Makes sessions longer and harder to maintain.
  • Less than 900,000 light pulses: Devices with lower pulse counts may be less effective over time.
  • Lack of cooling features: Can increase discomfort and the risk of skin irritation during use.
  • Poor usability: Devices without an LCD display can make it harder to track settings and use consistently.
